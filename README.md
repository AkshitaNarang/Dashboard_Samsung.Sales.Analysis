# Dashboard_Samsung.Sales.Analysis
A detailed analysis of Samasung Sales dummy dataset.
![Dashboard](dashpreview.png)
**Dashboard Analysis**
The organization generated a **total revenue** of **USD 185.39K** with **37K units sold**.
The **Samsung Galaxy Tab S9 FE** emerged as the **best-selling and most profitable product**, while the **Samsung Galaxy S21 FE** recorded the **lowest sales and profitability**.
**Black** was the** most frequently ordered color**, **followed by White**, indicating a **strong customer preference for neutral color** options.
Revenue and profit showed **consistent growth from 2021 to 2023**. However, a decline was observed in 2024, suggesting the need to review pricing strategies and sales initiatives.
The **majority of orders were successfully fulfilled**, reflecting a **high level of customer satisfaction**.
Based on the analysis, the organization reached its **Break-Even Point (BEP) in 2022**.
